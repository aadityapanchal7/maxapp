# Bonemaxxing Foundations

Bonemaxxing is the art of making your existing facial structure read sharper, healthier, and more balanced. It is not chasing dangerous hacks. It is improving the four layers of the face safely and consistently.

## The Four Layers of Facial Structure

- **Skeleton**: maxilla, cheekbones, jaw, chin, brow ridge, nose. Mostly genetic. Posture, breathing, dental health, and growth habits influence development — biggest window before sutures close.
- **Muscle**: masseters, neck, facial, postural muscles. Drive how strong or soft the face looks.
- **Soft tissue**: fat, water, fascia, skin tension. A sharp face can look soft if it's puffy.
- **Visual framing**: hair, brows, beard, makeup, lighting, camera angle. Same face, different read.

The goal is to improve all four layers safely.

## Aesthetic Hierarchy For Most Faces

1. Body fat low enough to show the underlying structure
2. Posture and tongue position pulled forward and up
3. Skin quality that lets light reflect cleanly
4. Debloat / lymphatic flow so morning puffiness doesn't hide the frame
5. Strategic grooming and lighting to amplify what's already there

## What Counts As Safe Bonemaxxing

Posture, sleep, nutrition, controlled chewing, neck training, nasal breathing, skin quality, debloat, grooming, and fraudmaxxing.

## What Does NOT Belong

- Hitting or smashing facial bones
- DIY jaw or chin pressure devices
- Steroid creams used off-label to "shrink" facial features
- Research chemicals like MK-677 for face changes
- Megadose supplement stacks
- Anything that causes bruising, numbness, swelling, or pain

If a method can injure your face, it is not bonemaxxing. It is injury.
